What are two benefits of having well-written commit messages and a good commit history?

- Stand out to prospective employers
- Have full context for each commit's decision

How many characters should the subject line of your commit message be?

- 50

BONUS: What are the seven tips for writing commit messages?

- 1. Use the feature where you can add a description to your one-line commit messsage
- 2. Don't go above 50 characters in your one-liner (subject)
- 3. Capitalize the first letter
- 4. But don't add punctuation at the end 
- 5. Write in present-tense imperative mood ("Clean this folder" or "Fix that bug")
- 6. Make sure your description's lines are 72 characters each
- 7. The description should explain what and why, not how
